Love Letter

Huhhh Kaha sai suru karu, chahe woh September ki raat ko tumhare teachers day kai pics pai feeda hone sai lekar December 1 ko handshake karte huye tumahre ankho mai khone tak. Uss din hi soch liya tha aab iski khusiyon ka karan aur dukh ka saathi ban na hai. December kai baad sai meri zindagi mai itne changes aaye jitne maine expect nahi kiye thai like college aane ka motivation, har samey khush rehna, tumhare khayalo mai khoye rehna, tumhare branch mai aane kai liye har hadd paar kardena and best part mera luck percentage badna after your entry to my life. Best part with you is travelling mann toh karta tha ki kabhi khatam hi naa ho bus humara safar chalta hi jaaye and Uncle Aunty toh Artist plus Sweet hai hi.

Haa thore Ups and Downs toh aayein hai, thori misunderstanding kai khataas hai but sort hone kai baad ki mithaas feels like naram rasgullo ka swaad. Presently jab yeh mai likh raha hu iss samey bhi thori misunderstanding chal rahi hai but i know hum isse bhi ubhar jaenge aur phir sai uss khatte mithe safar mai nikal jaenge.

Aur likh toh bahut kuch sakta hu par space nahi hai, at last ek hi cheez kahunga " Chahe jitni bhi Misunderstanding aaye mujh par believe karte rehna i promise tum apne decision pai humesa garvv karogi"
